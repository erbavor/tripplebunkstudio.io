  Frontline Brawler

Barbarian

“Raw force, no apologies.”

The most straightforward killer on the roster — high HP, high speed, highest base damage and low melee damage taken. Leap closes the gap, War Cry buys survivability through a burst window, and Wind Wheel punishes anyone who clusters. Her only weakness is being kited, so commit hard and don't let them breathe.

Abilities

    Launch forward and up, then crash down for 60 AoE damage in a 4m radius — your gap-closer onto squishy ranged classes.

    Spin in place dealing 15 damage every 0.4s in a 2.5m radius; drains stamina while active.

The Design

Barbarian

A fierce tribal warrior, skin streaked with mud and dried war-paint, crowned with voluminous coiled hair and woven beads. Layered fur, bone, leather and rough-iron armour over visible muscle, and a pair of brutal hand axes that do all her talking.
