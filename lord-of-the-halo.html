The Game

An arena-shooter Souls hybrid, in armour

Lord of the Halo fuses fast arena shooting with melee timing, dodge-rolls and class abilities. Equip a blade, a bow or raw elemental power and fight across a dozen modes.

Three Combat Styles

Cycle melee, ranged and elemental projectiles on the fly. Light/heavy combos, charged bows and seven elements.

Souls-like Defence

Stamina, guarding, parries and i-frame dodge-rolls reward precise, deliberate play.

Eight RPG Classes

From a teleporting Witch to a snowballing Vampire Necromancer — each plays nothing alike.

Play Your Way

Online, over LAN, or local split-screen — with bots to fill any lobby.

Signature Mode

RPG

The heart of Lord of the Halo. Lock in a class, then fight a free-for-all to the top — no two fighters built the same.

Before the match begins, every fighter locks in one of eight RPG classes. There are no neutral loadouts here — your class is your identity, your arsenal and your win condition. A teleporting Witch plays nothing like a snowballing Vampire Necromancer or a parrying Souls Knight.

  • Eight distinct classes

    Each with its own stats, weapons, abilities and signature colour — see the full roster below.

  • Class-aware kill feed

    Every clash is called out by class, so you always know who beat whom — and with what.

  • Snowball mechanics

    Classes like the Vampire Necromancer grow with every kill — damage, speed and lifesteal stack as you dominate.

  • Pure free-for-all

    No teams, no mercy. First to the kill limit — or the highest count at the buzzer — is crowned Lord of the arena.
