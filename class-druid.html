Druid

“The wilds answer his call.”

The Witch's grounded counterpart. Where she burns bright and brief, the Druid endures — a wood-elf shaman who channels the living forest through a gnarled antler-staff crowned with a green crystal. Slower and far sturdier than the Witch, he holds the field: snaring anyone who closes in, mending between fights, and answering fragility with roots, thorns and renewing light.

Abilities

  • Wildgrowth Bolts

    A steady stream of green nature magic. Lower burst than the Witch's barrage, but with none of her fragility tax — you can trade all day.

  • Entangling Roots 10s

    Burst roots from the ground where you aim, snaring and slowing everyone caught near the impact — punish anyone who tries to close the gap.

  • Regrowth 14s

    Channel the forest to heal yourself and nearby allies over a few seconds. Your answer to the burst you shrug off.

The Design

Druid

A rugged wood-elf shaman wrapped in living green. Tanned skin carries faint nature glyphs, long dirty-blonde hair and a full beard are woven with leaves and beads, and his eyes glow a luminous green. A leaf- and moss-covered cloak with a fur-and-feather mantle hangs over a bare, tattooed chest, finished with leather wraps, cords and bone charms — and a gnarled antler-wood staff that cradles a glowing green crystal.
