Abilities

  • Kill Scaling Passive

    Every kill grants +10% damage, +0.2 speed and 25 HP of lifesteal — stacking up to +50% damage. Get rolling and you become unstoppable.

  • Blood Feast 14s

    Drain all enemies within 8m for 30 damage and heal for the full amount dealt.

  • Summon Undead 25s

    Raise a skeleton (ranged) and zombie (melee) pair to harry your foes — up to four at once.

  • Bat Flight

    Take to the air in bat form to flank, chase or escape.

The Design

Vampire Necromancer

A confident dark-skinned vampire crowned with great bat wings veined in translucent magenta. Braided hair fades from black to hot-pink, a glossy black bodysuit pairs with thigh boots and opera gloves, and her fangs promise she does her healing the hard way.
