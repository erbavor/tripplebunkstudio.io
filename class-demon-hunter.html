The Design

Demon Hunter

A demon hunter whose entire body is semi-transparent, glossy dark-purple 'living slime' lit from within by a cosmic shimmer. Over it sits spiked black-and-gunmetal slime armour set with glowing violet gems; tendrils and drips trail from her hood and limbs, and her violet eyes pierce the dark.
