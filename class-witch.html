The Design

Witch

A tall, dark-skinned celestial elf sorceress wrapped in cosmic light. Violet energy glows through individual strands of her hair, her eyes burn lavender, and constellations are etched across her skin — finished with star-and-crescent ornaments and a sheer, star-flecked gown.
