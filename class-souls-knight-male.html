Souls Knight

“Timing is everything.”

The skill-expression class. A stamina bar gates every roll, block and swing, and mastering it makes you the best 1v1 duelist in the game — 240 effective HP and the lowest melee damage taken mean nobody wins a fair trade. Slow feet are the catch: ranged classes will try to kite, so pick your fights and parry the impatient.

Abilities

  • Dodge Roll Stamina

    A directional roll with invincibility frames — read the swing and roll through it for 30 stamina.

  • Guard & Parry Stamina

    Hold to block 50% of incoming damage; a guard timed to the first 0.3s parries the blow and stuns the attacker.

  • Sprint Slash

    Attacking mid-sprint triggers a forward lunging slash to catch runners.

  • Field Rest 45s

    Set down a field rest and recover at it to regenerate HP, shields and stamina between fights.

The Design

Souls Knight

A grim male dark knight in a gothic, Souls-like register. Fair skin, messy black hair and sharp brooding features, clad head-to-toe in black overlapping dragon-scale plate with dark-red accents bleeding through the scales. A tattered black cape lined blood-red hangs from his shoulders, and he carries an oversized black greatsword nearly as tall as he is.
