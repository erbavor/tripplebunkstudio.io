Mage

“Light from afar.”

The long-range artillery. The Mage is the slowest class on foot, but compensates with the second-highest projectile damage and a chargeable Fire Laser that deletes targets at range. Keep your distance, lean on the horse for repositioning, and never let melee close in — at 1.25× melee damage taken, you fold fast up close.

The Design

Mage

A wise, weathered male archmage in the classic grey-robed tradition. Long flowing silver hair and a full beard, sharp blue eyes, and layered grey robes patterned with a fine diamond-lattice weave beneath a long hooded cloak. He carries an ornate black staff crowned with a glowing crystal, with a sheathed sword at his hip.
