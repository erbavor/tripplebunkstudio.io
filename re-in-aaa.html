Every blade has a story. Every choice leaves a scar.

Re in AAA Video Game is a single-player and cooperative action-RPG built in Unreal Engine 5. Explore a living medieval world, shape a hero who is truly your own, and let your decisions ripple through its quests, its people and its fate.

Pillars

Built for the long road

Deep, deliberate systems — exploration, combat, growth and gear — that reward the hours you put in.

01

A Handcrafted World

A world worth wandering

From sun-struck mountain valleys to torch-lit streets and the dark below, every district is built by hand and seeded with secrets. Curiosity is always rewarded.

  • Explorable open landscapes and dense medieval towns
  • Hand-built and procedurally-extended dungeons to delve
  • Hidden lore, loot and encounters off the beaten path
02

Melee & Magic

Steel in one hand, spells in the other

A reactive, ability-driven combat system lets you blend weapon work with magic. Chain attacks, time your defence and unleash signature abilities to break any foe.

  • Fluid melee, ranged and spell combat
  • Gameplay-ability-system powered skills and effects
  • Distinct enemy types and faction-driven encounters
03

Progression & Identity

Become the hero you choose

Create your character, then grow them down the paths you favour. Skill trees, abilities and stats let you build a brawler, a battle-mage, a ranged hunter — or your own hybrid.

  • Full character creator with custom appearance
  • Branching skill trees and unlockable abilities
  • Persistent saves that remember your journey
04

Armour & Weapons

Gear that changes the fight

Outfit your hero with modular armour and a deep arsenal. Swap loadouts to suit the dungeon ahead, and look the part while you do it.

  • Modular, layered medieval armour sets
  • A wide weapon roster across combat styles
  • Inventory and equipment that carry real weight
05

Living Quests & Dialogue

Stories that answer back

A branching dialogue and quest system drives a world that reacts to you. Talk, choose, and watch the consequences follow your party from town to dungeon.

  • Branching conversations with meaningful choices
  • Layered main and side quests with journals
  • NPCs, companions and factions with their own agendas

Play Together

Co-op, on one couch

  • 2–4 Player Split-screen

    Bring the whole adventure to a single screen — drop in with keyboard or controllers.

  • Press to Join

    Grab a controller and jump in; each hero keeps their own look, gear and progress.

  • Shared Story

    Dialogue, quests and saves are routed per player, so everyone stays part of the tale.
