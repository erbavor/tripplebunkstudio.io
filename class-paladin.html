Paladin

“The wall that won't fall.”

The immovable object. With the highest effective health in the game and a bubble that heals through sustained damage, the Paladin is brutally hard to put down. Simple to play, nearly impossible to kill: hold your ground, swing the hammer, and outlast everyone on the field.

The Design

Paladin

A radiant holy paladin sworn to an oath of blindness. Blonde hair spills from a white hood and veil over a dark blindfold; ornate white-and-gold plate inlaid with deep-blue gems sits over a long tabard — devotional, knightly and luminous.
