Ranger

“Never heard, never seen.”

The ambusher. The Ranger's strength is choice — engage at range, vanish into cover, or slip behind the fight unheard and unseen. Balanced stats keep every option open, but 1.15× melee damage taken means brawling isn't the plan: hit, disappear, and hit again from somewhere new.

Abilities

  • Bow & Blade

    The only class that starts with both ranged and melee weapons — open from range, finish up close.

  • Active Stealth 10s

    Toggle near-invisibility (and hide your nameplate) for up to 8 seconds; it breaks the moment you attack.

  • Passive Stealth

    Fade automatically when you stand in shadow or tall grass near trees.

  • Silent Step Passive

    Passive — your footsteps are near-silent, so enemies never hear the ambush coming.

The Design

Ranger

An agile woodland ranger built for mobility and ambush. Light layered leather and cloth, a deep hooded cloak, and a bow always within reach — equally at home loosing arrows from cover or drawing a blade when the fight gets personal.
