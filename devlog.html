Welcome to the devlog

This is home base for development updates from Tripple Bunk Studios. We’re a small team building two games at once — a fast, class-based fantasy arena (Lord of the Halo) and a story-driven co-op action-RPG (Re in AAA Video Game) — and we want to build them in the open.

Expect work-in-progress footage, feature breakdowns, art and design notes, and the occasional honest post-mortem. Video updates go up on our YouTube channel and get embedded right here.
